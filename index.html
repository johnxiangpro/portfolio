ABOUT ME

As a consulting professional, John is passionate about helping organisations make better decisions, generate sharper insights and unlock their full potential from data.

John's experience spans across strategy development (product, distribution, growth, operations), risk management and the implementation of large-scale transformation projects; with a focus on the financial services industry.

In addition to his functional expertise, John is well versed in data techniques spanning from data exploration, analysis and storytelling through visualisation; with demonstrated experience in tools such as SQL, Alteryx, PowerBI and Tableau.

Outside of work, John is an avid swimmer, photographer and foodie.
